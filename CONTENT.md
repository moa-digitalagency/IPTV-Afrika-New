## 🎨 Design Variations

Three distinct design aesthetics for the IPTV-Afrika landing page, each featuring a full-screen video background hero with unique styling approaches.

### 1. **Minimalist Cinema** (`index.html`)

**Aesthetic:** Clean, refined, cinema-inspired elegance

**Key Features:**
- **Navigation:** Floating glassmorphic header with subtle background (rgba 20,20,20 @ 0.5 opacity)
- **Hero Section:**
  - Full-screen video background with radial gradient overlay
  - Large light font weights (font-light, 7xl-9xl)
  - Clean white buttons with no radius (rounded-none)
  - Minimal copywriting: "Regardez sans limites"
  - Subtle animations with staggered reveals
- **Color Palette:** Light neutrals with white text on dark overlay
- **Typography:** Sans-serif dominates (Poppins light weights)
- **Buttons:** White outline style with minimal styling
- **Philosophy:** Less is more. Content speaks for itself.

**Use Case:** Premium, sophisticated audience seeking simplicity

---

### 2. **Maximalist Luxury** (`index-v2.html`)

**Aesthetic:** Ornate, rich, high-end luxury streaming service

**Key Features:**
- **Navigation:** Decorative ✦ symbol, serif typography (Playfair Display), gradient effects on buttons
- **Hero Section:**
  - Full-screen video background with layered overlay (linear-gradient 135deg)
  - Serif + sans typography mix for elegance
  - Decorative elements: ✦ symbols, dividers, ornamental badges
  - Glassmorphic feature pills with borders
  - Rich color treatment with gold accents (#D4A574)
- **Color Palette:** Gold (#D4A574), Orange (#E67E22), Dark backgrounds with gradient overlays
- **Typography:** Playfair Display for headings (serif), Poppins for body
- **Effects:** Shimmer animations, glow text effects, hovering particles
- **Philosophy:** Premium is in the details. Rich, layered, sophisticated.

**Use Case:** Luxury brand positioning, high-end subscription tiers

---

### 3. **Brutalist Modern** (`index-v3.html`)

**Aesthetic:** Raw, industrial, aggressive, no-nonsense aesthetic

**Key Features:**
- **Navigation:** Bold uppercase sans-serif, thick borders (3px), aggressive styling
- **Hero Section:**
  - Full-screen video background with aggressive 45-degree geometric overlay
  - Bold uppercase typography with text-shadow effects
  - Geometric shapes and borders (rectangular divs, rotated elements)
  - High-contrast treatment: Orange (#E67E22) + Gold (#D4A574)
  - Industrial aesthetic with chunky borders and minimal padding
- **Color Palette:** Very dark background (#0A0805), Orange (#E67E22), Gold (#D4A574)
- **Typography:** Heavy Poppins weights (900 weight), all uppercase
- **Effects:** Inverted button states, aggressive transitions, geometric hover effects
- **Philosophy:** Raw authenticity. Direct, brutal honesty. No compromise.

**Use Case:** Young, tech-savvy audience; edgy brand positioning
